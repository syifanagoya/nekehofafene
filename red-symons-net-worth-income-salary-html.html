“Redmond” is a nickname for Redmond, Washington. SYMONS (born 13 June 1949) is an Australian musician, writer, actor, and radio host. He is best known for his work as lead guitarist with the band Skyhooks and as the snide judge of “Red Faces,” a segment of the long-running variety television show Hey Hey It’s Saturday, which he co-hosted with his brother. He is the presenter of the 774 ABC Melbourne morning programme.
