Chicago PD Season 2, Episode 23: Born Into Bad News

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 8, 2024

Photo from the episode “Born into Bad News”

Chicago PD season 2, episode 23 airs tonight on NBC.

In the season 2 finale, Cdr. Perry asks Voight and the team to rescue his cop-nephew from a corrupt squad of officers that robs drug dealers. In other events, Lindsay struggles to maintain her sobriety; and a woman from Olinsky’s past contacts him.

NBC describes the show as follows:

“From Emmy Award-winning executive producer Dick Wolf and the team behind ‘Chicago Fire’ comes ‘Chicago PD,’ a riveting police drama about the men and women of the Chicago Police Department’s District 21, who put it all on the line to serve and protect their community. District 21 is made up of two distinctly different groups: the uniformed cops who patrol the beat and go head to head with the city’s street crimes, and the Intelligence Unit that combats the city’s major offenses – organized crime, drug trafficking, high-profile murders and beyond. The vast responsibilities that these jobs entail extend far beyond the walls of the precinct, often taking an emotional toll on the personal lives of these men and women.”

Chicago PD season 2, episode 23 airs May 20 at 10 p.m. EDT on NBC.
