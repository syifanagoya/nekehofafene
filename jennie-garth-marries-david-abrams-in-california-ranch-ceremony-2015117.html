Jennie Garth Marries David Abrams in California Ranch Ceremony

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, March 6, 2024

Pop the bubbly! Jennie Garth married her fiancé, David Abrams, at her Los Olivos, Calif., home in the Santa Ynez Valley on Saturday, July 11, Us Weekly can confirm.

The Beverly Hills, 90210 alum and actor-producer exchanged vows on her six-acre ranch and were feted by close family and friends.

Us broke news in January that Garth, 43, and Abrams were dating. The couple got engaged in April and celebrated the milestone with a romantic trip to Mexico.

Garth told Us in May that she was very hands-on with wedding planning. "I’m working with an amazing designer for the dress which I’m very excited about. We’re sort of creating that together," she said at Motrin's "Make It Happen" campaign event on May 4. "I like to call the shots and when I know what I want, the best person to make it happen usually is me."

In addition, the Jennie Garth Project star noted that she was in the middle of landscaping her backyard, the backdrop of her beautiful ceremony. "I do Pinterest sometimes," she said of her inspiration. "My house is very white and bright and light."

Garth's hard work eventually paid off, but more importantly, she got to share her special day with daughters Luca, 17, Lola, 12, and Fiona, 8. "They are going be my wedding party," she told Us of her girls, whom she shares with ex-husband Peter Facinelli. "It's going to be special and sweet."

This is the third marriage for Garth. She and Facinelli, 41, finalized their divorce in June 2013 after 11 years of marriage. For his part, the Twilight actor popped the question to actress Jaimie Alexander in March. Garth was also previously married to Daniel Clark from 1994 to 1996.
