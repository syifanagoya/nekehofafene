Mila Kunis News - Us Weekly

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 26, 2024

Name:Mila Kunis

Hometown:Los Angeles, California

Birthday:August 13, 1983

About

Seven years after emigrating from Ukraine in 1991, Mila Kunis made her TV debut on That ‘70s Show at age 14. Kunis starred as Jackie Burkhart on the Fox sitcom from 1998 to 2006 and has voiced Meg Griffin on Family Guy since 1999. She nabbed her breakout film role in 2008’s Forgetting Sarah Marshall, going on to appear in Black Swan (2010), Friends with Benefits (2011), Ted (2012), Bad Moms (2016), The Spy Who Dumped Me (2018) and Luckiest Girl Alive (2022).

For her role in Black Swan, Kunis received nominations for a Golden Globe Award and a Screen Actors Guild Award. She married her former That ‘70s Show costar Ashton Kutcher in 2015. The couple share two children: daughter Wyatt and son Dimitri. Kunis has expressed support for her native country throughout the years, notably fundraising for Ukrainian refugees in 2022.
