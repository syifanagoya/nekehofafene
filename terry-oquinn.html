Terry OQuinn

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, April 5, 2024

O’Quinn in 2019, photo by kathclick/Bigstock.com

Birth Name: Terrance K. Quinn

Place of Birth: Sault Ste. Marie, Michigan, U.S.

Date of Birth: July 15, 1952

Ethnicity:
*father – Irish
*mother – English, Scots-Irish/Northern Irish

Terry O’Quinn is an American actor. He is known for playing The Stepfather in the horror film series and John on the show Lost, among many other roles across film and television.

His father was from Wisconsin. His mother was from Alabama. Terry was raised in Rudyard, and then Newberry, Michigan. He has two children with his former wife Lori O’Quinn.

Terry’s paternal grandfather was Patrick Quinn (the son of Arthur Quinn and Mary Jane Lee). Patrick was born in Adams, Wisconsin. Arthur was born in County Offaly, Ireland, the son of Patrick Quinn and Sarah Smith. Mary Jane was born in Wisconsin, the daughter of Irish parents, Patrick Lee, who was from Galway, and Sarah Amanda Moore.

Terry’s paternal grandmother was Margaret Catherine/K. McKillip/McKillup (the daughter of Daniel McKillip and Johanna/Joanna Walsh). Margaret was born in Yellowstone, Wisconsin, to Irish parents. Daniel was born in Antrim, the son of John McKillip and Sarah Wiley. Johanna was born in Waterford, the daughter of Michael Walsh and Katherine Devine.

Terry’s maternal grandfather was Lee/Lester Otis Martin (the son of Robert Haywood Martin and Josey Cleo Tilder Whiddon). Lester was born in Alabama. Robert was the son of Haywood Francis Martin and Sarah Ann Tharpe. Josey was the daughter of Edward Taylor David Whiddon and Nancy Jane Dykes.

Terry’s maternal grandmother was Willie Mae Riley (the daughter of Zinnamon/Zimmerman/Zimerson Harvey Riley and Mittie Roella “Ella” Hicks). Willie was born in Alabama. Zinnamon was the son of John Riley and Mary Jane Woodham. Mittie was the daughter of John Adelbert Hicks and Martha Elizabeth “Betty” Etheridge.
